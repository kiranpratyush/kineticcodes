The most important attribute of good customer service, is the fast response time . Hence at kinetic Codes we follow the customer first approach to keep our average response time as 15 minutes for any communication sent to us .

Kinetic Codes always focusses on providing their services to clients all across the globe within their respective timezone's business hours . However , in case of emergencies / critical issues . our development team and business team also serve instantly irrespective of time . Our customer support can be conducted 24 *7.

Each and every project adds a milestone in this splendid journey of Kinetic Codes , hence the pricing entirely depends on the services required and scope of the project .
There are two types of pricing present
- Fixed price
- Hourly
Fixed price of the project is decided by our business team which masters in breaking down the requirements in milestones generally executed using waterfall methodology. Each milestone is of a fixed amount. Once all milestones are cleared , the project is marked as complete after which the support plan is activated .
Hourly projects can include project enhancements support contracts or projects developed using Agile Methodology .

We can proudly say that all our products are always bug free and smooth, as per the client's requirements. However the products developed at Kinetic Codes are tested thorougihly and handed over to client which when delivered and handovered has the warranty of 45 days. During this timespan, our team will be responsible for fixing all the bugs if they occur. After this timespan, the support plan is activated.

The project is divided into milestones by our business analysts after which , when the deliverables are tested succesfully by us and the client. We generate an invoice against the milestone which needs to be cleared in not more than 7 days by payment methods communicated by Kinetic Codes business team which can be Paypal, Wire Transfer or Western Union .
Please note the funds should always be transfered into company's account and none of our employees can or are supposeed to resquest funds in their personal account.

Along with the expert team, one business manager and one project manager will be assigned to the project. For any escalations related to technical details of the project will be handled by the project manager . However , all billing related information or queries will be handled by the business manager .

Well structured and sustainable management is the key for success of any project and Kinetic Codes aces in it with the use of Jira and Asana for managing the Product Development Lifecycle .

All our employees share critical information to all our clients by using organizations email. However for daily meetings, we use Google Meet and Zoom. All brainstorming and feedback sessions are hosted on slack workspace of Kinetic Codes team .

Kinetic Codes always focusses on Data Security regarding the information of clients and the projects by opting firm measures like signing an exhaustive Non Disclosure agreement with both clients and our employees and using our end to end encrypted Information Management System .
