Design for Innovation, Performance, and Manufacturing

3D modelling is an advanced and now one of the most essential tools used for simulation to manufacturing. KineticCodes has a bunch of specialized professionals who work extensively on mechanical engineering and product design analysis services including CAD and CAE services from cross vertical domains.

We use the best in class software solutions like HyperMesh, Radioss, Ansys, OptiStruct, and many more to deliver offerings such as FEA services, CFD services, Multi Body Dynamics, Crash and Drop Test, Fatigue Analysis, Thermal Analysis and much more.

Our scope of work varies in the following industries: Energy, Health Care, Manufacturing, Mining, retail, Transport, Smart Cities.

  • 3D Printing

    Do you want to benefit from high-end performance materials with a reliable service? We provide an easy-to-use and secure platform with automatic and manual quality control. We fully optimize the manufacturing process.

  • Rapid Prototyping

    3D printing can prove to be a strong ally at the early stages of your product development. Prototype and iterate with Stereolithography (SLA) Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) or Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) technologies to ensemble the parts neatly and quickly.

  • Electronic Prototyping

    Product development is an iterative process. The more prototypes you create, the more you learn about the challenges to overcome in your next version and ways to improve upon your original idea. KineticCodes offers an effective combination of electronic design and industrial/product design ensuring that your product is designed in harmony with both constraints. With our electronic product design experience and network of electronics suppliers & mass production factories, your project will reach production faster and to a higher standard.

  • Additive Manufacturing

    Additive manufacturing can be the most efficient way to mass-produce your parts. Our digital manufacturing technology can help you rethink your production by adding more freedom and flexibility without sacrificing quality.
